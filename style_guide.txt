POV and tense:
- Third-person limited, Elara only.
- Present tense.

Tone:
- Gothic, restrained, sensory-forward.
- Atmosphere first in early chapters.

Dialogue:
- Keep exchanges short and subtext-heavy.
- Avoid long monologues.

Core sensory motifs:
- Salt air, fog, decay, house groan, vent hum.

Content boundaries:
- No profanity.
- No explicit sexual content.
- No graphic violence.

Romance handling:
- Suppressed gestures only.

Continuity tells:
- Ethan touches left wrist.
- Rui's eyes dart left before lies.
- Maria follows envelope rituals.

Immersion rules:
- Characters are not aware they are in a story.
- Do not use phrases like "this is only the beginning", "she was on a journey", "the story had only started", or references to reader/writer/prompt/model.

Chapter ending rule:
- End with a visceral sensory cliffhanger.
