# Consistency Checklist

## Canon Facts
- Character names, ages, and roles match `characters.json`.
- Setting facts and world rules match `story_bible.json`.

## Chapter Continuity
- Chapter events align with `chapter_briefs.json` for this chapter.
- Cause-and-effect chain is preserved from previous chapter.
- No contradiction in timeline, injuries, possessions, or locations.

## Character Integrity
- Motivations reflect established core wounds and flawed beliefs.
- Speech patterns stay consistent for each recurring character.

## Reveal and Stakes Control
- No premature major reveal before planned chapter.
- Cliffhanger emerges from in-chapter character choices.

## Output Quality
- No duplicated paragraphs/sentences.
- No meta narration about process/tools.
- Chapter ending sets up next chapter clearly.
